Hands-On Experience: My Learning Journey

initiation internship

july 2022

My first professional experience was a valuable introduction to the workplace, at Attijari Bank. While the focus wasn’t on technical skills, it taught me essential lessons about work culture, discipline, and the importance of punctuality. I learned how to effectively communicate with colleagues, adapt to a structured environment, and prioritize tasks, which helped me develop a strong sense of responsibility and professionalism. These lessons have shaped my approach to work and are integral to my growth in any role.

summer internship

june - july 2023

During my internship at Orange, I built my first functional application with the support of my supervisors and senior colleagues. I developed a simple sales contracts retrieval app using Spring Boot,ReactJS , and SQL Server for internal use within the company. This experience introduced me to important frameworks and provided a hands-on learning opportunity, allowing me to improve my coding skills, collaborate in a team, and gain practical knowledge of the development process.

End of studies internship

february - May 2024

My most recent internship at LunarTC was my end-of-studies internship, where I worked on a leasing credit application submission and treatment platform using Spring Boot, Angular, and MongoDB. It was an invaluable learning opportunity that allowed me to deepen my knowledge by working on a real-life project. Additionally, I gained exposure to new design tools like Figma, further enhancing my skill set and giving me hands-on experience with both development and design aspects of a project.
